Science fiction is how I explore ideas bigger than code. The best sci-fi makes you question assumptions.

## Favorites

- **Foundation** by Isaac Asimov — the original "big picture" thinking
- **Dune** by Frank Herbert — politics, ecology, and power
- **Solaris** by Stanislaw Lem — what if we can't understand the alien?

## Currently Reading

*Blindsight* by Peter Watts — hard sci-fi about consciousness and what it means to be intelligent.
